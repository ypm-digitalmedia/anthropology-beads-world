Catalog Number: YPM ANT 295500

Title: Large white jade bead in the form of a cong with a grid of 11x5 raised bumps on each of the four sides, grouped in threes. Said to represent heaven and earth. The circumferential carved decoration is found in the Western Zhou period, and becomes very important in the late Eastern Zhou or Warring States period. Ca. 1100-771 BCE.; YPM ANT 295500
